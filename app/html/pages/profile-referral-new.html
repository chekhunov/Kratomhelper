how it works?

  • Сopy your referral link

  • Send your referral link

  • Get paid

    Get money for the fact that your referral has registered and bought a product from your link

If a new customer registers and makes an order by following your link, you get 10% of this customer’s order amount. If this customer creates his/her own link and invites another customer (who makes an order), you get 5% of the latter’s customer’s order amount. And so on up to the third degree. The order in the third degree brings you 2% of an order amount. To get the money, the order must have a “sent” status.

If the customer decides to return the order, it will not be your concern (as far as we are ready for such expenses). It should be noted that the money is real (not bonus), so you are able to get this money out any way you prefer.
